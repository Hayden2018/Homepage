Dialogcraft

As an AI enthusiast, I would like to have maximum control over my interactions with AI like ChatGPT. Therefore I developed Dialogcraft - a feature-rich desktop app that directly interfaces with OpenAI API. This app offers complete control over the model parameters for each conversation and comprehensive history editing ability. Additional features such as text streaming and automatic conversation naming further enhance the user experience.

Music Flip

Recognizing the challenges piano players face when trying to turn sheet music pages during a performance, I created Music Flip - an innovative Android app designed to help musicians flip PDF sheet music pages using simple head gestures, such as nodding or winking. This app provides a hands-free solution to a common problem, allowing musicians to focus on their performance.

Book Summary

I read books from time to time. When I encounter a book that interests me a lot, I write a summary highlighting the ideas that I find most valuable.

Summary on The Photographer's Eye

The Photographer's Eye by Michael Freeman is a comprehensive guide that explores the art and technique of photographic composition. First published in 2007, this book breaks down the essential components that contribute to creating striking and effective photographs. My summary selectively emphasizes the book's most practical ideas for finding great photos, placing less focus on artistic discussions that lack clear advice.
